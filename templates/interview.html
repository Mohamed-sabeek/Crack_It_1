Interview Questions
Instructions for TNPSC Group 1 Interview Preparation

I have provided 10 important questions for the major departments that are most commonly asked in the Group 1 interview.

  • Don't Memorize: You don't need to remember every word.
  • Understand the Concept: Focus on learning and understanding the idea behind each question.
  • Get an Overview: Gain knowledge about all these questions so you know what topics may come up.
  • Build Confidence: Knowing the concepts will give you courage and clarity during the interview.
  • Speak Clearly and Honestly: Even if you don't know the exact answer, show understanding and logical thinking.
  • Connect to Real Life: Relate answers to practical administration, governance, and public service.
  • Practice Delivery: Try to explain answers in your own words so it sounds natural.
Summary: Learn, understand, and be confident. Knowledge + confidence is more important than memorization.
